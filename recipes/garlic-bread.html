Roasted Garlic Bread

Roasted garlic bread is a delicious side to any Italian pasta dinner.

How to Make Roasted Garlic Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 Heads of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 (1 pound) loaf of Italian bread
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons grated parmesan
  • 1 tablspoon chopped fresh parsley

The Process

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Slice the tops off of garlic heads, so the tip of each clove is expposed. place garlic heads, cut side up, on baking sheet; drizzle tops with olive oil.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until garlic is soft, about 30 minutes.
  4. Set the oven to broil.
  5. Slice bread loaf in half horizantally; place cut-side up on baking sheet.
  6. Squeeze Garlic cloves from their skins into a medium bowl. Stir in butter, Parmesan cheese, and Parsley until well blended. Spread onto the cut sides of bread.
  7. Broil bread until toasted, about 5 minutes.
